Sentillum

Description

Sentillum is an invisible and untouchable ghost. To put it simply he does not occupy any matter, however he is capable of creating a body with random metal objects around him. Depending on which materials he has picked, he can look quite different but most of his forms are humanoid.

It is aggressive but will not attack unless he needs to or when he is in a complete advantage. His strength depends on the quality of the materials that make up his body. In his weakest state he is just as strong as a human person and in his strongest he is comparable to Godzilla. He doesn't need to feed or reproduce.

Story

After a failed mission Mister Jones Lang has his body mostly erased but attempted to use the flames of an Ambustum to revive himself. It is assumed that this procedure transformed him into Sentillum due to his first sighting being moments after this.

He tries to hide himself from the public and us so he rarely is ever sighted. He still has the intellect from before and uses it fully. He always starts with small scrap parts and then hunts for better and rarer metals to grow and improve his body. To achieve his goal he will kidnap and kill anybody necessary.

When having achieved his final body he has communicated with us and stated that he plans on unifying with all the creatures in the establishment. He has been dealt with heavy artillery multiple times but we have yet to find a way to completely stop him.

He has hinted that through his mutation from human to Sentillum he has gained some knowledge but his goal is completely selfish and it has nothing to do with it.

Species

Sentillum is the only specimen existing from his species. The process that gave birth to him has been repeated but he has apparently absorbed the new specimens. As previously stated it is unknown if he can die or if he is even alive during his ghost form but all that changes when he begins absorbing metal.

After absorbing the first bit of metal his body will follow normal human laws. He has to feed on energy to keep his body going and when part of his body is destroyed, he will feel pain as normal. This also means that when upgrading he can't just remove body parts and replace them with newer ones without feeling massive amounts of pain.

His combat capabilities are quite high. As mentioned before he starts rather weak but thanks to his intellect he can almost always grow strong enough to need anti-tank missiles. In his small humanoid form he is agile and fights with sharp objects plus any weapons he can find and in his giant form he is slow but has massive firepower. He can also heal by absorbing metal.
