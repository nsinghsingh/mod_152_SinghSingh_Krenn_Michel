Fulgur

Description

Fulgurs are quadruped creatures made out of clouds and high currents of electricity. Their size and shape can vary between a small dog and an entire mountain. The have large glowing mouths and glowing eyes which make them easy to spot from afar.

They are highly hostile and will fight anything that they encounter to the death. They are also capable of flight. If ever spotted one should seek refuge in the closest building possible and aim to stay out of it's sight.

Story

Fulgurs are mythical creatures which have been mentioned in ancient records and drawings. These documents hint to a peaceful relationship between humans and Fulgurs but an unknown event occurred which worsened this relationship and led to the disappearance of Fulgurs up until now.

All civilians that have encountered this creature have died except for those who had the smallest version. As of right now a special combat unit has been deployed to kill any medium and above sized Fulgurs in order to avoid a massacre.

Species

Fulgurs are extremely fast, capable of flight and hurt their enemies by electrocuting them or when big enough with water attacks. Since they are made of gas no physical weapon can damage them. The only way to defeat them is by reducing the temperature or chemical weapons.

The bigger they are, the more dangerous all of their attacks are. A simple electric shock could transform into a deadly 3m wide laser by 200m sized Fulgurs. However they can only survive in mist and non-polluted areas. Because of the high pollution all over the world the maximum size a Fulgur can reach is expected to be around 200m. Fulgurs do not consume energy or reproduce. The materialize in a cloud and fight until they shrink to nothingness.
